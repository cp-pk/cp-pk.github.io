Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 20, 2025

PK Labs LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website https://www.pk-labs.org (the "Website") and the mobile application PouchKinetics (the "App"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our Website or use our App.

1. PouchKinetics Mobile Application

No Data Collection: The PouchKinetics app operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or usage information. All data you enter (including nicotine usage logs, personal settings, and health information) remains exclusively on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third parties.

Local Data Storage: Any data you create within the app is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. This data is never transmitted outside of your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

No Analytics or Tracking: The app does not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or tracking technologies.

No Third-Party Services: The app does not integrate with any third-party services that could access your data.

2. PK-Labs.org Website

For our website, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: If you contact us via partnerships@pk-labs.org, we collect your name, email address, and any information you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personal data about your interactions with the Website, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, through standard web server logs.

3. How We Use Website Information

Information collected from our website is used to:

  • Respond to your inquiries or partnership requests
  • Improve the Website's functionality and user experience
  • Analyze Website usage through aggregated, anonymized data

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • With service providers who assist in operating the Website, bound by confidentiality agreements
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others

5. Cookies and Web Technologies

Our Website may use standard web technologies such as cookies for basic functionality. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. The PouchKinetics app does not use cookies or web technologies.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. For the PouchKinetics app, since no data leaves your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The PouchKinetics app is designed for adult use only and relates to nicotine usage tracking.

8. International Data Transfers

Any website data we collect may be transferred to and processed in the United States. The PouchKinetics app processes all data locally on your device, so no international transfers occur.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Data portability
  • Objection to processing

For the Website, contact us at partnerships@pk-labs.org to exercise these rights. For the PouchKinetics app, since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data and can delete it by uninstalling the app or using the app's built-in data management features.

10. California Privacy Rights

California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since the PouchKinetics app collects no personal information, these rights apply only to Website interactions. Contact us for more information about exercising these rights.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
